Craizie starter

Begin with an AI use inventory.

List every place AI creates, recommends, decides, communicates, or acts in your business. For each use, name the data it receives, the person accountable, and what happens if the output is wrong.

A sane governance roadmap

  1. Inventory AI uses
  2. Classify customer and business impact
  3. Name an accountable human
  4. Set data and action boundaries
  5. Test important behavior
  6. Document disclosure and response
