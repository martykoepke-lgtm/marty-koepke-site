3 ways to improve AI visibility

These recommendations are designed as implementation moves. They explain where the content belongs, what to add, and what success should look like.

1

Add proof beside the claims you want AI to repeat

AI needs nearby evidence it can connect to the claim. A testimonial on a separate page is less useful than proof placed directly under the claim it supports.

Where

Primary service page below the offer explanation. Add a shorter proof band on the homepage and credentials on the About page.

How

Create a Claim / Evidence / Source block. Each row should include one claim, one proof asset, and a source link or named example.

Success

AI can answer why the business is credible without flattening the claim into vague category language.

Example: Claim: "We improve AI readiness." Evidence: "Client scan moved from Hidden to Discoverable after schema, proof, and fit updates." Source: link to case note or before/after screenshot.

2

Add explicit use cases and not-fit language

AI recommendations improve when the site states the buyer situations where the business is the right answer.

Where

Primary service page, offer/pricing page, FAQ, consultation booking page, and a short homepage section near the CTA.

How

Add four blocks: Best for, Common triggers, Not a fit for, and Choose this when. Use buyer language, not internal positioning language.

Success

AI recommends the business for specific buyer prompts and avoids wrong-fit recommendations.

Example: Best for founder-led service firms with a real offer and a public website. Not a fit for ecommerce brands looking for paid media management.

3

Restructure important pages with AI-readable headings

Headings are not decoration. They tell AI what each section means and which passages answer which questions.

Where

Homepage, top service page, About page, FAQ, methodology/process page, and any page in the main navigation.

How

Use one H1 and H2s like Who this is for, What this measures, What you receive, Proof, Use cases, and FAQ. Add schema and llms.txt after the page structure is clear.

Success

AI can extract identity, offer, proof, and buyer fit from named sections instead of guessing from scattered copy.

Example: Replace "Our Services" with "AI Readiness Audit for Founder-Led Service Businesses," then structure the page around buyer questions.
