## 2. The formal positioning statement

**Classic format (for internal clarity and investor/partner conversations):**

> For small and midsize businesses, solo founders, and practices who are becoming invisible in AI search but don't have a marketing team to fix it, **Practical Informatics** is a *done-with-you AI visibility partner* that measures where you actually stand, explains it in plain English, remediates it alongside you, and then proves the number moved. Unlike AI-visibility software that hands you a dashboard, or agencies that sell a retainer in marketing jargon, Practical Informatics pairs a transparent, Lean-validated methodology with two decades of domain-translation experience — turning AI visibility from a black box into something you understand and control.

**Plain-English version (for the website):**

> AI tools are quietly becoming how your customers find and choose. If you're not showing up in those answers, you're invisible — and most businesses have no idea where they stand or what to do about it. I measure exactly how visible you are across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google's AI answers, explain what it means without the jargon, fix it with you, and re-measure to prove it worked.

---

## 3. The category — what you are (and aren't)

You are a **done-with-you AI visibility partner.**

- You are **not** a software subscription. Software measures; it doesn't act, interpret, or stand beside you.
- You are **not** a traditional SEO/GEO agency. Agencies sell retainers in marketing language to companies that already have a marketing function.

You sit in the gap between them: the trusted expert who *translates and implements* for the owner who isn't a marketer.

---

## 4. Who it's for — and who it's not

**Built for:**
- Small and midsize businesses, local service businesses, solo founders, and small practices.
- Owners who are growth-minded and ready to invest in showing up — not those just trying to survive the month.
- People who want to *understand* what's happening, not just be handed a score.

**Not the right fit for:**
- Enterprises with in-house marketing teams who just need a measurement dashboard (the tools serve them well).
- Anyone looking for guaranteed rankings or overnight results — this is honest, measured work, not magic.

---

## 5. Your four differentiators

1. **Translation, not dashboards.** The whole point is plain English. You get what's happening, why it matters, and what to do — without needing to become a marketer.
2. **Done *with* you, in your niche.** Not a fix list you're left to implement, and not a faceless retainer. Real remediation, alongside someone who knows your world — healthcare-adjacent and local.
3. **A transparent, provable method.** Every number is explainable. The methodology is Lean-validated and repeatable — the opposite of a black-box score you can't question.
4. **Proof it moved.** A baseline, the work, then a re-measure 60–90 days later that shows the change. You don't just get a report; you get evidence.

| | **AI Visibility Tools** *(Otterly, Peec, Profound)* | **Traditional GEO/SEO Agencies** | **Practical Informatics** |
|---|---|---|---|
